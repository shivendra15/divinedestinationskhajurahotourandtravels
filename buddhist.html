The Buddhist Pilgrimage

Bodhgaya, Sarnath, Kushinagar, Rajgir, Nalanda & Lumbini 🇳🇵

日本からのお客様へ — For Our Japanese Guests

These pilgrimage journeys are designed with the highest standards of comfort, safety, and spiritual respect. Every route follows the sacred footsteps of the Buddha, guided by certified experts who understand the profound significance of each site for Japanese Buddhist traditions.

BP-01 | 5 Days

The Enlightenment Trail

悟りへの道 — The Path to Awakening

"Walk the ground where Prince Siddhartha became the Buddha." A focused pilgrimage through the most sacred sites of Buddhism within India.

Sacred Sites

  • • Bodhgaya — The Mahabodhi Temple & Bodhi Tree
  • • Sarnath — Site of the First Sermon
  • • Rajgir — Vulture Peak & Bamboo Grove

The Divine Detail

Private meditation session under the Bodhi Tree at dawn, before the crowds arrive.

Bodhgaya & Sarnath Express

Highlights

  • • Bodhgaya — Enlightenment & Meditation

BP-05 | 4 Days

The Nalanda Wisdom Journey

智慧の旅 — In Search of Ancient Knowledge

"Walk the corridors where scholars from across Asia gathered for a thousand years." A focused journey exploring the intellectual heritage of Buddhism — the world's first residential university and its surrounding monastic landscape.

Academic & Spiritual Sites

  • • Nalanda — UNESCO Ruins of the Ancient University
  • • Rajgir — Vulture Peak & Hot Springs
  • • Nalanda Museum — Rare Buddhist Artifacts
  • • Xuanzang Memorial — Honoring the Chinese Pilgrim

The Divine Detail

Private guided meditation at Vulture Peak at sunrise, the exact location where the Buddha delivered the Heart Sutra (般若心経).
