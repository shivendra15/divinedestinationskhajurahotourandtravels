The Rock-Cut Masterpieces

Prehistoric India

先史時代のインド — Bhimbetka & Sanchi

"Stand before 30,000-year-old cave paintings and the Great Stupa commissioned by Emperor Ashoka himself." A journey into the very dawn of human artistic expression.

Highlights

  • • Bhimbetka — UNESCO rock shelters with prehistoric art
  • • Sanchi Stupa — Ashoka's Buddhist masterpiece
  • • Bhopal — The City of Lakes

The Vijayanagara Empire

ヴィジャヤナガラ帝国 — Hampi

"Wander through the ruins of a city that was once richer than Rome." Hampi's boulder-strewn landscape holds the remains of one of the world's greatest medieval empires.

Highlights

  • • Vittala Temple — the iconic stone chariot
  • • Royal Enclosure — the seat of empire
  • • Tungabhadra River — coracle boat ride

  • • Bijapur — Islamic domes and arches
